About Khweng Village

Khweng is a picturesque village in the Ri-Bhoi district of Meghalaya, known for its lush greenery and rich cultural heritage. It is home to skilled weavers of Eri silk (Ryndia) and boasts sustainable practices in food, water conservation, and handicrafts.
